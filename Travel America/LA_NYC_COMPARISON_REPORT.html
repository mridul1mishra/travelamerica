Bottom line

The two cities are not "same structure, different content." They use two fundamentally different architectures. Making LA match NYC is a structural rebuild of most LA pages, not a content swap.

What "make them match" actually means

Because NYC is the template, aligning LA requires, per page:

  1. Replace city-hub.module.css with a per-page CSS module copied from NYC's.
  2. Decompose the monolithic LA client into the same ./components/ sub-component tree NYC uses (Category 2 — 7 pages).
  3. Rebuild Category 3/4 LA pages' markup to NYC's bespoke layout.
  4. Normalize wrapper (<main> → <>) and <Header /> props.
  5. Decide what to do with LA-only extras (best-areas sections, itinerary page) — currently flagged.

This is realistically a page-by-page port. Only content (text, images, neighborhood data, links) would then differ.
